## How to read this

- **Healthy ceiling**: highest target rps where p99 < 500ms, errors < 1%, achieved ≥ 70% of target. This is your sustained capacity headroom.
- **Knee**: first target rps where p99 doubles vs the lowest tested step (or ≥100ms). Where latency starts degrading.
- **Peak achieved**: raw throughput the server delivered, regardless of target.
- **Breaks between X and Y rps**: the step at X was healthy, Y was not. The true ceiling is somewhere in [X, Y].
